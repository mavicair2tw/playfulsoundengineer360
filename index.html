The Artist
William Chen

With drones and cameras, he traces invisible lines across the sky — capturing the fleeting dances of light and shadow. With music, those visions weave into soundscapes: fragments of memory, longing, and hope.

Every creation is an offering — a bridge to people he may never meet, and a quiet tribute to his father, Tommy Chen, whose timeless works still guide his vision to see beyond what the eye alone can hold.
